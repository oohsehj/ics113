Destiny

Destiny is a MMOFPS. That stands for Massive Multi-player First Person Shooter, with some RPG elements. It is created by Bungie with Activision publishing the game. This game follows you the guardian of the last safe city on earth. In the game, with the help of your friends, battle the forces of "darkness". You also have three classes you can choose from warlock, titan, and hunter.

    PROS:
  • Great Soundtrack
  • Armor designs are awesome
  • Great side stories
  • Excellent gunplay
    CONS:
  • DLC needed to make the game have a coherent story
  • Microtransactions
  • The classes don't feel diverse or different from each other
  • Players need to leave the game in order to understand the lore cards you can obtain ingame.

Assassins Creed Rouge

Assassin's Creed Rouge is a third person stealth adventure game. You play as Shay Patrice Cormac. You will learn of his story and how he plays an important role of the ideology that is used in Assassin's Creed Rouge.

    PROS:
  • Good story
  • Boat mechanics are great.
  • Compelling plot
    CONS:
  • Terrible graphics
  • Some of the characters do not stand out well or are not that good
  • Some of the side quest/activities are not satisfying

Fire Emblem Fates

In Fire Emblem Fates you play as Corrin who was rasied by the Nohrian royalty but is of Hoshidian blood. In the game you will have to make one of three choices. This choice will effect what storyline you play. So do you side with the Nohrians who are invading your homeland, side with Hoshidians who you barely know, or do you choose neither and forge your own path.

    PROS:
  • Good story
  • Game play is great
  • Some ingame functions are great for customizing
    CONS:
  • You need to buy all of the versions in order to understand the complete story
  • Some characters are either annoying or just not able to live up to other fire emblem characters
  • Story line is very cliche

Pokemon Sun/Moon

Pokemon Sun and Pokemon Moon is a rpg game where you plays as the protagonist. In normal pokemon games that are not spin offs you would collect eight gym badges and would challenge to league. In Pokemon Sun and Moon changes it up by having you complete trials instead of gathering badges. Another different thing between Pokemon Sun and Pokemon Moon is that you don't have to use HMs or hidden moves. These (for those who have never played a pokemon game) are moves that had to be taught to some of your pokemon in order to progress the game. Besides these differences Pokemon Sun and Pokemon Moon is the same as any other pokemon game there is.

    PROS:
  • Okay story
  • Inovates in some areas of the game
    CONS:
  • Tedious tutorial that holds you hand
  • No compelling rival
  • Some of the pokemon designs are terrible

Pokemon Alpha Sapphire/ Omega Ruby

Pokemon Alpha Sapphire and Omega Ruby is a remake of the original Pokemon Sapphire and Ruby for the Gameboy Advanced SP. The story in this game is you play as the protagonist collecting the eight gym badges in order to beat the elite four and become the pokemon master. The evil teams are the same as they were in the originals. In case you forgot they are Team Aqua and Team Magma respectively.

    PROS:
  • Pokemon designs are great
  • Story is alright
  • Has new mechanics
    CONS:
  • Too much water routes
  • There is a need for two bikes in order to access some of the routes

Pokemon SoulSilver/HeartGold

Pokemon SoulSilver and Pokemon HeartGold is a remake of Pokemon silver and gold (respectively) for the gameboy color. Like in every other pokemon game that is not a spin off you play as the silent protagonist. You face the evil team rocket(or the evil team of the region), while you are trying to gather eight gym badges (or in this case sixteen because you can access another region). When you collect all the eight gym (or sixteen) badges you can challenge the elite four and their champion. If you win you become the champion of the league for that region.

    PROS:
  • Great rival
  • Alright story
  • Your pokemon come out of their pokeball and follow you
    CONS:
  • Tedious second region

Fire Emblem Awakening

Fire Emblem Awakening is a rts or real time strategy game. You play as the main character Robin, which you can name him/her yourself, who goes on adventures with his friend Chrom. As you play and progress the story you find yourself fighting an evil god who wants to destroy everything in the world. With your companions you have to stop this evil god while finding our a terrible truth.

    PROS:
  • Great story
  • Likable characters
  • Good combat system
    CONS:
  • Too much DLC
  • The story needed to be a little longer

Call of Duty Black Ops 1

Call of Duty Black Ops 1 is a fps or a first person shooter that has you play as Alex Mason a solider of the American army. The game is mostly played in flashbacks as to where and when you did any of the events. This game is also set in the cold war. The game is an action packed thriller that will keep you on the edge of your seat.

    PROS:
  • Great story
  • Likable characters
  • Made improvements to the Nazi Zombies mode
    CONS:
  • MICROTRANSACTIONS

Call of Duty Black Ops 2

Call of Duty Black Ops 2 is the continuation of Call of Duty Black Ops 1. In this game you play as Alex Mason and his son David Mason. The story in the game is that you have to stop the terrorist Raul Menendez during the second Cold War. In the game you have access to old and new tech. There are also new ways you can tackle the objectives in this game.

    PROS:
  • Great story
  • Likable characters
  • Has you able to make different choices
    CONS:
  • MICROTRANSACTIONS
  • Only start with on horrible zombies map while the rest you have to buy.

Halo Reach

Halo Reach is set in the year 2552 where humans have gained the ability to travel to different planets. You play as Noble 6 a spartan who was recently tasked to a spartan team called Noble Team. There task is to defend Reach from the Covenant. The Covenant is an alien race that will stop at nothing to see there great journey come complete. The story is about the death of Noble team and the fall of Reach to the Covenant.

    PROS:
  • Great story
  • Great combat system
  • Has great multilayer and map creation system
    CONS:
  • Some characters lack development
